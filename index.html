Our Services

Boundary & Title Analysis

We specialize in boundary determination, record research and title review. Every opinion is built on thorough examination of public and private records, careful field inspection, and evidence analysis. Our workflow includes researching unrecorded and recorded documents, collecting field evidence, comparing it with title research, and determining property lines, encroachments and easements【869768299838938†L42-L59】. This detailed process produces clear, defensible opinions supported by proper documentation.

Legal Descriptions & Exhibits

Accurate legal descriptions are critical for conveyance and development. As a Licensed Professional Land Surveyor, we prepare metes and bounds descriptions, easement descriptions, and exhibit maps using surveying, trigonometry and knowledge of legal conventions【510672966198381†L105-L110】. California law recognises that the preparation of legal descriptions is the business of the licensed surveyor【377806050238465†L158-L167】; our work ensures your descriptions are clear and comply with regulatory requirements.

Survey Consulting & Mapping

Before you build or develop, we review title reports and surveys to uncover potential issues. A comprehensive survey review compares your title commitment with survey maps to identify encroachments, easements and discrepancies【747761045999638†L208-L233】. We provide CAD mapping and record research to support architects, engineers, attorneys and developers who need accurate base data for design and planning.

Selective Field Survey Services

Our field work is selective and targeted. When required, we conduct boundary retracements, topographic surveys and monument recovery. Boundary surveys involve client consultation, governmental and title research, field inspection and measuring, evidence collection and comparison, and preparation of a final map filed with the county【970966365877398†L116-L129】. We take on field assignments that align with our consulting ethos and deliver maximum value with minimal disruption.
