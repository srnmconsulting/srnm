Services

Boundary Research & Boundary Review

Review of deeds, maps, title documents, adjoining records, prior surveys, and related evidence to identify boundary issues, controlling calls, record conflicts, and matters affecting the property.

Legal Descriptions & Exhibit Plats

Preparation of legal descriptions and exhibit plats for easements, rights of way, fee conveyances, and related land matters, written for clarity, consistency, and locatability.

Title, Easement & Encumbrance Review

Review of title reports, Schedule B items, easements, rights of way, encumbrances, and recorded exceptions to help clients understand property constraints and survey-related risk.

Survey Consulting & Technical Review

Technical review and consulting support for attorneys, architects, engineers, commercial owners, and real estate representatives who need clarity before legal, design, acquisition, or development decisions move forward.

Commercial Due Diligence Support

Focused support for commercially relevant and title-sensitive matters requiring careful review of boundaries, title, easements, legal descriptions, and survey scope before decisions move forward.

Survey QA & Mapping Support

Independent review of third-party survey documents, legal descriptions, exhibit plats, and mapping for clarity, consistency, and technical alignment.

About

SRNM Consulting Inc. is a California land surveying and boundary consulting practice focused on title-sensitive, commercially relevant, and technically complex property matters.

The firm is led by a California Professional Land Surveyor with a background in geomatics and surveying, commercial and right-of-way related work, private and public-sector experience, and an MBA from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

Additional credentials include FAA Part 107 remote pilot certification. Experience spans California and Chicago, with work informed by both technical surveying practice and business-minded judgment.

Mission

To provide clear, defensible surveying and boundary consulting that helps clients understand land, reduce risk, and move projects forward with confidence, integrity, and practical judgment.

Service Area

Field-related project support, when accepted, is limited to Los Angeles County and San Bernardino County.

SRNM Consulting does not provide field work in Orange County.

Non-field services, including boundary research, legal descriptions, title review, easement review, and survey consulting, may be provided on a selective basis outside Los Angeles County.
