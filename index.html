Services

Boundary & Title Analysis

Analysis of record title, deeds, maps, and historical documents to support boundary determination. Work includes review of easements, rights of way, and record discrepancies to develop defensible boundary conclusions.

Legal Descriptions & Exhibits

Preparation of metes and bounds descriptions, easement descriptions, and supporting exhibits. Descriptions are written for clarity, consistency, and compliance with California standards and professional practice.

Survey Consulting & Mapping

Review of title reports, ALTA surveys, and boundary documents to identify conflicts, encroachments, and gaps in record information. CAD mapping and research support provided for design, entitlement, and legal coordination.

Selective Field Survey Services

Field surveys are performed selectively when required to support boundary analysis or project needs. This may include monument recovery, boundary retracement, and limited topographic data collection.

About

SRNM Consulting Inc. is operated by a Licensed Professional Land Surveyor in the State of California. The practice focuses on boundary analysis, title interpretation, and preparation of legal descriptions, with experience in both public agency and private sector projects.

Work is performed with an emphasis on accuracy, defensibility, and clarity. Each project is approached through careful review of record evidence, evaluation of existing surveys, and application of established boundary principles.

Mission

To provide precise, defensible boundary analysis and survey consulting services that support sound decision-making for property, design, and development.

Goals

  • Deliver clear and defensible boundary and title analysis based on record and field evidence.
  • Prepare accurate and consistent legal descriptions suitable for conveyance and recordation.
  • Support architects, engineers, attorneys, and developers with reliable mapping and technical review.
  • Maintain compliance with California laws governing land surveying practice.
  • Provide focused, high-quality services rather than high-volume work.

Service Area

SRNM Consulting Inc. primarily serves Los Angeles County, with a focus on the San Fernando Valley, including Tarzana and surrounding communities.

Services are available throughout the greater Los Angeles region. Select projects in San Bernardino County may be undertaken through coordinated partnership.

Services are not currently offered in Orange County.
