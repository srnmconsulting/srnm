Firm Overview

A focused surveying practice built around clarity, judgment, and defensible deliverables

SRNM Consulting Inc. is a California land surveying practice serving clients who need careful review of boundary, title, easement, and land documentation issues. The firm is structured to provide practical technical support on matters that often require more than drafting alone.

Our work emphasizes record research, legal descriptions, exhibit plats, title-sensitive mapping, and selective survey support for projects where clear interpretation and disciplined documentation matter.

Core Services

Services tailored to title, boundary, commercial, and pre-development needs

SRNM provides focused support where technical survey judgment, mapped exhibits, and record-based review are essential to moving a project forward responsibly.

Boundary Analysis and Record Research

Record-based review of deeds, maps, adjoining references, and boundary evidence to support informed decisions before design, acquisition, or dispute escalation.

Legal Descriptions and Exhibit Plats

Preparation and review of metes and bounds descriptions, easement descriptions, grant exhibits, and supporting survey graphics for land-related documents.

Title Report and Easement Review

Review of title materials, Schedule B items, easements, rights-of-way, and related encumbrances to help clarify what affects a parcel and what may require further attention.

Mapping and Survey Document Review

Review of plats, parcel maps, record maps, legal exhibits, survey drafts, and supporting land documents for consistency, clarity, and technical completeness.

Commercial Survey and Due Diligence Support

Survey-related support for commercial properties, title-sensitive acquisitions, development planning, and consultant coordination where land information must be organized and clearly communicated.
